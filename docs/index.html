Welcome to Rhody Radar!

What is Rhody Radar?

Rhody radar is your one-stop shop for everything you need at the University of Rhode Island.
Here, you have access to every club and event currently running at URI. From there, you can learn more information about each club/event as well as save the ones you like for future reference. You can also search for only clubs or only events specifically. This allows you to keep track of all the goings-on at URI from the palm of your hand!

How to Use Rhody Radar

Welcome Screen

When directed to the Welcome Screen, click "Get Started".

Sign Up/In Screen

Here, you are presented with the sign up screen. If you already have an account, click "Sign In Instead" at the bottom and login.

Upcoming Events/Clubs

Once logged in, you will now be shown the list of upcoming events offered at URI. If you wish to see the list of clubs offered instead, click "Clubs". If you wish to see your account settings, click "Settings". If you wish to see the clubs or events you follow, click "Following".

Event/Club Details

While scrolling through the list of upcoming events or clubs, click on one of them to learn more about it. The example image shown below is the "Considering Change" event. Here, you can view the location, time, and description of that particular event or club.

Settings Screen

Clicking the "Settings" button will direct you to this screen. Here, you are able to reset your password, sign out of your account, and change your profile settings - such as reseting your followed events or clubs.

Following Screen

Clicking the "Following" button will direct you to this screen. Here, you are able to view the clubs and events you have followed. To go back to list of upcoming events or clubs, click "Go to Feed".

Club Connections

For certain clubs, like the Billiards Club for example, you are able to visit their Instagram page by clicking the Instagram icon. You are also able to reach out to clubs you are interested via email by clicking the "Email" button.
